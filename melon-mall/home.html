The Scoop on Juice

Why Melons?

Well the thing about melons is that they are amazing, and you can't deny that! Furthermore, they come in many different colors AND many different flavors. Who couldn't love that variety? They are healthy, sweet, and quenching-- perfect for a healthy dessert, a hot afternoon, or really any time, day or night, rain or shine.

Now That's Some Water for Ya

Did you know over half of our bodies are made up of water? It's pretty crazy, but that just goes to show how important water is for us. Well, guess what else has an even GREATER water ratio than us? Watermelons!! This juicy, delicious fruit is not made up of 60%, not 70%, but 92% water! Yeah, you heard me right. What? You don't have any watermelon? Go get some you dehydrated, unhealthy fool!

The Secret of Watermelon

I know, I know. There's no way they could possibly get better, right?? Well, don't lose your rind, but it absolutely can! If you've already had one, then you should know by now, but if not... what are you doing still here?? Go get one now!! Anyway, all the melons are good--canteloupe, muskmelon/honeydew, watermelon--but none of them compare to watermelon. Now, this IS personal preference, but I must explain. Watermelon has that CRUNCH, and the most water amount than any of them. It's delicious, full of water, and yep, you heard it, CRUNCHY. Bet you didn't know it had that crunch, huh? Well, now you know the secret of watermelon :)
